Functions of Eye

Eyes are the sensory organs that allow you to see. Human eyes capture visible light from the world around and turn it into a form your brain uses
to create your sense of vision. The eye is a complex organ that is made up of many different parts, each with its own. Eyes constantly adjust the amount of
light thry get in. Eyes help us to see colour separating light into its different wave lenghts and detecting the relative intensity of each wavelength.
