PORSCHE

Porsche 911 or Pfister is a two-door 2+2 high performance rear-engined sports car introducedin september 1964 by Porsche AG of Stuttgart,Germany.It has a rear-mounted flat-six engine and originally a torsion bar suspension.The car has been continuously enhanced through the years but the basic concept has remained unchanged.The engine were air-cooled until the introduction of the 996 series in 1998.
The 911 has been modified by private teams and by the factory itself for racing, rallying, and other forms of automotive competition.It is among the most sucessful competition cars.
